Jason Reeves:
#FindYourFolly

I am a sales professional with a rich background in the automotive industry. I have sold cars, rented cars, consulted about cars, sold commercial trailers, and then consulted about cars some more. I would not describe myself as a "car guy," but I do spend a great deal of energy staying ahead of the volatile car market here in the states.

I grew up in northern California, but finished high school in Salt Lake City, UT. I also attended university at the University of Utah. After 4 years of study, I received a degree in history with a sociology minor. I had intended to teach, but realized I did not possess the patience for it.

My passion is travel, tequila, and golf. I enjoy all three, preferably at the same time. Norman Schwartzkopff and Theodore Roosevelt are the two greatest Americans that ever lived. I celebrate birthdays with Big Macs and margaritas. I celebrate Christmas with Prime Rib and mashed potatos. I am well known for being trustworthy and capable.
